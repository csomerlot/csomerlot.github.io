Rain Water Cistern

  1. Provides irrigation water to gardens
  2. Collects runoff from Barn roof
    1. 2500 sf
      1. 1/4” event yields almost 400 gallons
    2. secondary benefit = keeps ground around outside of barn from getting marshy
  3. Main Storage tanks
    1. elevated to provide head to fill transport tank
    2. Discharge header
      1. Connect to inline transfer pump for filling transport tank
      2. Flex hose at one end allows control of fill level, overflow protection, and winter drain
  4. Field storage tanks (65 gall x 2)
    1. located upslope of gardens
      1. needs low pressure emitters
    2. kept inside ply “doghouse” to avoid algal growth and for appearance
    3. main shutoff valve and hose quick-connects
    4. open top for filling
  5. Transport tank (65 gall)
    1. strapped into tractor trailer bed
    2. filled via gravity from main storage tanks
    3. fills field storage tank via gravity
      1. park tractor upslope
      2. large diameter fill hose
    4. fills field storage tank via pump
      1. use tractor to power 12V transfer pump

Spinning Wheel

  1. quill spindle style
    1. simplicity of construction
    2. flexibility in yarn type
    3. can also wind shuttle bobbins and make center pull balls
    4. speed
    5. no orifice threading
  2. single treadle
    1. simplicity
    2. dual action
    3. Can be set at any angle from wheel, to adjust position of wheel relative to spinner
  3. Primary drive wheel
    1. 20” diameter
    2. Solid laminated wood
      1. Dimensionally stable
    3. Axis position is adjustable
  4. Quill Drive
    1. Driven by direct contact with drive wheel
    2. Held in place between drive wheel and roller bearings
    3. 80:1 ratio for quill speed of ~250rpm
