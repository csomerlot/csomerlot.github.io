Solar water heater

  1. For preheating hot water supply
  2. Run hot water loop to faucets for instant hot water
  3. Collector design
    1. Insulated box with black metal roofing
    2. Copper pipe loop collects heat in insulated box, transmits heat to water in holding tank
    3. Brine as transfer liquid, so it won't freeze
  4. Controller design
    1. python-based microcontroller
    2. logic: if temp in collector > temp in heat exchanger, then run pump
  5. Heat Exchanger design
    1. Modified propane tank, with insulation
    2. Collector fluid runs through replacable copper coil inside tank
    3. pressure/heat relief valve
