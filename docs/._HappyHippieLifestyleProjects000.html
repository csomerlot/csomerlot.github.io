    Dimensions:
    12" dia by 6.5' tall
    Volume:
    35 gallons
  • tap fittings into pipe walls for filter inlet/outlet
  • Injection port on pump effluent for feeding and air bubbles
  • Integral water heater
  • Stored on top of tank to prevent leaks
